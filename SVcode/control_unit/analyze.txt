The testbench results show that the Control Unit generates the correct control signals for
all required RV32I instructions. The outputs for RegWrite, ImmSrc, ALUSrc, ALUControl,
MemWrite, ResultSrc, and PCSrc are all consistent with the designed control table.

In particular, branch instructions are handled correctly through the interaction between the
Zero flag and the PCSrc selection logic. The BLT and BGE instructions also operate properly
by using the SLT-based comparison together with Zero. Although some don't-care fields are
assigned fixed values during simulation, they do not affect the functionality of the
processor. Overall, the Control Unit meets the coursework requirements and behaves correctly
in the tested scenarios.
